PERSONAL LOAN AGREEMENT

This Personal Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on January 15, 2024, between ABC Lending Company, a corporation organized under the laws of the State of [State] ("Lender"), and Michael Johnson, an individual residing at 456 Oak Avenue, City, State 12345 ("Borrower").

1. LOAN AMOUNT
The Lender agrees to lend to the Borrower the principal sum of Twenty-Five Thousand Dollars ($25,000.00) ("Principal Amount").

2. INTEREST RATE
The loan shall bear interest at a fixed rate of eight and one-half percent (8.5%) per annum, calculated on the outstanding principal balance.

3. REPAYMENT TERMS
The Borrower shall repay the Principal Amount plus interest in equal monthly installments of Seven Hundred Fifty Dollars ($750.00) each, due on the first day of each month, beginning February 1, 2024, and continuing for a period of thirty-six (36) months.

4. LATE PAYMENT CHARGES
If any payment is not received by the Lender within ten (10) days after its due date, the Borrower shall pay a late charge of Twenty-Five Dollars ($25.00) for each late payment.

5. DEFAULT RATE
Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the interest rate on the outstanding principal balance shall increase to twelve percent (12%) per annum.

6. COLLATERAL
As security for the repayment of this loan, the Borrower hereby grants to the Lender a security interest in the following collateral: 2020 Honda Civic, VIN: 1HGCV1F3XLA123456.

7. DEFAULT
The following shall constitute Events of Default under this Agreement:
a) Failure to make any payment when due;
b) Bankruptcy or insolvency of the Borrower;
c) Any material misrepresentation made by the Borrower in connection with this loan.

8. ACCELERATION
Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the Lender may, at its option, declare the entire outstanding principal balance and accrued interest immediately due and payable.

9. COLLECTION COSTS
In the event of default, the Borrower shall be responsible for all costs of collection, including but not limited to reasonable attorney's fees and court costs.

10. PREPAYMENT
The Borrower may prepay this loan in whole or in part at any time without penalty.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [State].

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.
