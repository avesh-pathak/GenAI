RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2024, between John Smith, Landlord ("Landlord"), and Jane Doe, Tenant ("Tenant").

1. PREMISES
The Landlord hereby leases to the Tenant the residential property located at 123 Main Street, Apartment 4B, City, State 12345 ("Premises").

2. TERM
The term of this lease shall be for a period of twelve (12) months, commencing on January 1, 2024, and ending on December 31, 2024.

3. RENT
The Tenant shall pay to the Landlord the sum of Two Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($2,500.00) per month as rent for the Premises. Rent shall be due on the first (1st) day of each month.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
Upon execution of this Agreement, the Tenant shall deposit with the Landlord the sum of Two Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($2,500.00) as security for the faithful performance of the Tenant's obligations hereunder.

5. LATE FEES
In the event that the Tenant fails to pay rent when due, a late fee of Fifty Dollars ($50.00) shall be assessed for each day the rent remains unpaid after the fifth (5th) day of the month.

6. UTILITIES
The Tenant shall be responsible for the payment of all utilities including but not limited to electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash collection, and internet service.

7. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Landlord shall be responsible for major structural repairs and maintenance of the Premises. The Tenant shall be responsible for minor repairs and maintenance, including but not limited to light bulb replacement and minor plumbing issues.

8. PETS
No pets shall be allowed on the Premises without the prior written consent of the Landlord. If pets are permitted, an additional pet deposit of Five Hundred Dollars ($500.00) shall be required.

9. QUIET ENJOYMENT
The Tenant shall have the right to quiet enjoyment of the Premises, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

10. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving thirty (30) days written notice to the other party. In the event of early termination by the Tenant, the Tenant shall be liable for all rent due through the end of the lease term.

11. DEFAULT
If the Tenant fails to pay rent when due or otherwise breaches any term of this Agreement, the Landlord may terminate this Agreement and take possession of the Premises.

12. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State].
